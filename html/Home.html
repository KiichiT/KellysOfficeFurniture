Kellys Office Furniture, Sydney

Kelly's Office Furniture supplies the widest selection of office furniture at the best prices. We stock the largest range of commercial, home office and educational furniture including desks, boardroom tables, workstations, reception desks, chairs, ergonomic chairs, storage solutions, compactus, screens, partitions, whiteboards accessories and much more. We also have an extensive range of secondhand and used office furniture. Our aim is to make shopping easy with a complete selection of furniture from the most trusted brands. Our main office furniture showroom is in Dee Why and we also have an office in Brookvale specialising in used office furniture. Large buying power, local manufacturing and direct importing allow us to supply the most competitive prices in the Sydney. Our business is split into two divisions; our Direct office furniture sales and our Corporate office furniture services, dedicated to office design and commercial office fit outs in the Sydney area.
